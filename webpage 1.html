Minecraft Guide

About Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang. The game allows players to build with a variety of different blocks in a 3D procedurally generated world, requiring creativity from players. It also includes exploration, resource gathering, crafting, and combat.

Controls

  • W: Move forward
  • A: Move left
  • S: Move backward
  • D: Move right
  • Space: Jump
  • Shift: Sneak
  • E: Open inventory
  • Left Click: Break block/Attack
  • Right Click: Place block/Use item

Crafting Recipes

Here are some basic crafting recipes:

  • Crafting Table: 4 wooden planks
  • Stick: 2 wooden planks
  • Pickaxe: 3 wooden planks/cobblestones/iron ingots/gold ingots/diamonds + 2 sticks
  • Sword: 2 wooden planks/cobblestones/iron ingots/gold ingots/diamonds + 1 stick
  • Furnace: 8 cobblestones
  • Chest: 8 wooden planks

How to Use Different Tools

  • Pickaxe: Used to mine stone and ores. Higher-tier pickaxes (iron, diamond) can mine more valuable ores.
  • Axe: Used to chop wood and wooden items faster.
  • Shovel: Used to dig dirt, sand, gravel, and other soft materials faster.
  • Sword: Used to deal more damage to mobs and other players.
  • Hoe: Used to till dirt blocks into farmland for planting crops.
